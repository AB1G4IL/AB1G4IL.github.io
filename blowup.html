How To Have a Succesful Preppy Account

If you want to get on the fyp, you just need to follow a few simple steps.

Heres the list!!

  • A big part of going viral is having good content!
    Use the page "How to Have Good Conetent" as a
    reference! :))

  • Always use popular hashtags that are revelant to your post! For
    example, use #fyp or #preppy. But DONT use too many hashtags!!

  • As tempting as it seems, DO NOT self promote your account on others
    posts!! It's annoying, and people will block you! Also, it won't do much for
    your accoutnt.

  • Make sure that the audios you use have loads of views. Never use your own
    audio, and try your best to use a sound that a lot of people have used recently!
